About me

As a military veteran, I've seamlessly blended my passion for music production, DJing, coding, and web development into a dynamic career.

Specializing in techno and house music, I craft electrifying mixes and emotive soundscapes that captivate audiences.

Leveraging my background in technology in the computer programming space, I have learned to get a solid grasp on producing music through several digital technologies.

Fueled by boundless curiosity and resilience, I push the boundaries of creativity and technology.
